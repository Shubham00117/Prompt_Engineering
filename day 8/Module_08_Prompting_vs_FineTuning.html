2Ô∏è‚É£ When Prompting Fails

You hit the "Prompt Ceiling" when:
1. Complexity: The instructions are 10 pages long and the model forgets steps.
2. Style: You need a very specific tone (e.g., "Speak like Shakespeare") perfectly every time.
3. Latency: Your prompt is 5,000 tokens long (slow/expensive) for every request.

Method What it modifies Effectiveness
Full Fine-Tuning Updates ALL weights (Billions). Most powerful, extremely expensive.
LoRA (Low-Rank Adaptation) Adds tiny "adapter" layers. Agile, cheap, almost as good as full finetuning.
Soft Prompts / Prefix Tuning Adds "virtual tokens" (vectors) to the input. Like a super-charged system prompt learned from data.
